LPG VAPORIZER (KEV Series)

LPG VAPORIZER (KEV Series)

Electric Heated and Water Bath Type

FEATURES

  • Electric heating with water bath type(Heating medium: water / Heat source: electric heater)
  • Design pressure : 1.77MPa(17.7bar)
  • Explosion proof : Class I, Division 1, Group D & Ex d IIB T4
  • Electric power : 220V 1P 50/60Hz, 220V 3P 50/60Hz, 380V 3P 50/60Hz, Etc
  • High quality and long-life electric heater
  • Safety devices
    • Prevention of liquid LPG overflow with reset by mechanical float
    • LPG Pressure safety valve : 1.76MPa(17.6bar)
    • Overheat protection by water thermal switch
    • Low water cut-off for heater by water level switch
  • Water temperature & Level gauge
  • LPG Temperature & Pressure gauge
  • Power status lamp to check power on-off condition
  • Electric power connections both the left side and right

OPTIONS

  • Additional device for liquid LPG overflow prevention by solenoid valve and water thermal switch
    • In case of electric power outage and water low temperature
  • Control panel to check condition of vaporizer in the office
  • Explosion proof type lamp for solenoid valves open-close
  • Explosion proof type lamp for electric heaters on
  • Explosion proof type Emergency lamp for water high temperature
  • Explosion proof type selector switch for Vaporizer on-off
  • Stainless steel heating coil
  • Additional device for liquid LPG overflow prevention by LPG level LPG level switch
